Titles are assigned based on your Elo
Which can be determined by saying !rank
Titles indicate exceptional performance within our ranking system and thus some recognition is in order

CHAMPION [CHAMP]
Awarded to the player with the highest Elo on the server. This player deserves recognition and respect for attaining such a feat.

GRANDMASTER [GM]
The title Grandmaster is awarded to players above 2500 Elo. Apart from Champion, Grandmaster is the highest title a player can attain.

INTERNATIONAL MASTER [IM]
The title International Master is awarded to players above 2200 Elo. International Masters are considered strong players.

NATIONAL MASTER [NM]
The title National Master is awarded to players above 2000 Elo.National Masters are good, but not strong players.

CANDIDATE MASTER [CM]
The title Candidate Master is awarded to players above 1900 Elo. Candidate Master ranks below other titles. This indicates the player is not yet a master, but could potentially become one.
